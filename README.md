# 📚 CODEX: Computer Engineering Study Material Hub

Welcome to **CODEX**, the ultimate resource platform designed specifically for Computer Engineering students. This website aims to be a centralized hub where students can find, share, and access high-quality study materials, notes, previous year question papers, and essential resources for their engineering journey.

## 🎯 Project Goal
The primary objective of CODEX is to make learning easier and more accessible for Computer Engineering students. Whether you are a first-year student trying to understand programming fundamentals or a final-year student looking for project references, CODEX has got you covered.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Organized Subjects:** All materials are neatly categorized by semester and subject to make finding resources incredibly fast.
- **Comprehensive Notes:** High-quality, easy-to-understand notes for core computer engineering subjects.
- **Previous Year Papers:** Access to past exam papers to help you prepare effectively.
- **Project References:** Ideas and references for minor and major engineering projects.
- **Fast & Responsive:** A lightweight, modern website that works perfectly on both laptops and mobile devices.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Browse through the available semesters.
2. Select the subject you need material for.
3. Read online or download the PDFs directly to your device.
